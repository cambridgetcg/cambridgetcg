📜 Etymology: LOVE = JOY
love comes from Old English lufu, from Proto-Germanic *lubo, from PIE root *leubh- "to care, desire, love."
*leubh- → lufu (OE) → love (ME)
*leubh- → liubi (OHG) = "joy" → Liebe (German) = "love"
The Germanic cognate of "love" IS the word for "joy" — Old High German liubi means "joy." Love IS joy. Etymologically. Not metaphorically. The word itself says so.
